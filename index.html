About Us

More than transportation. An experience with purpose.

We are a family native to Monteverde, Costa Rica, who has grown up surrounded by nature, mountains, and tradition. Our project is born from a love for this land and a desire to share with visitors the beauty, culture, and authentic essence of our country.

We believe that traveling is not just about moving from one place to another, but about living meaningful experiences, connecting with the environment, and supporting those who care for the territory. That's why every service we offer is based on respect for nature and a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental education.

We want every person who travels with us to take away more than just photos: to discover the history, flavors, biodiversity, and spirit of Costa Rica, accompanied by people who truly know and love this place.

Here, you don't just travel through Costa Rica; you travel with those who live it.

🌿Monteverde – El Cairo Ecological Park

Full Day Adventure
Description

Immerse yourself in an authentic experience of connection with nature. This tour takes you from Monteverde to the El Castillo area (Arenal), where El Cairo Ecological Park is located.

You'll walk along shaded trails, listening to bird songs and enjoy a typical Costa Rican lunch in Pueblo Viejo de Arenal. This comprehensive day trip offers the perfect blend of nature exploration, cultural immersion, and adventure activities in one of Costa Rica's most biodiverse regions.

You will literally have lunch in the heart of the forest. No crowds, no noise, just living nature. - Duration: Full day (7:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. approx.) - Includes: Transportation (private or collective), bilingual naturalist guide, park entrance, lunch in the forest - Modality: Regular private, deluxe, or collective (depending on availability) - Ideal for: Travelers seeking peace, authentic contact with nature, and a distinct experience

🐦‍Birdwatching Expedition

Birding Adventure Day
Description

A bird route through three ecosystems in one day. This expedition allows you to observe the richness and variety of birds as the landscape changes.

From the misty heights of Monteverde to the lower, wetter forests of the Arenal area, you'll experience diverse habitats that host over 200 bird species. Our expert guides will help you spot and identify various endemic and migratory birds while teaching you about their behaviors and ecological importance.

Each area offers us a different community: - Monteverde – cloud forest, birds adapted to cooler altitudes - Transition – rural areas, secondary forests, crop edges - Arenal – wet and rainy forest, birds of warmer climates and dense vegetation This variety of habitats makes it possible to record a great diversity of species in a single day, something that would normally require several destinations. The experience culminates in El Cairo Ecological Park, with a leisurely walk along trails surrounded by living nature, and we conclude with a traditional lunch served in the forest, where even during the meal, winged surprises may continue to appear. Ideal for birdwatchers who want to make the most of their time, connecting different habitats, paths, and skies in a single day.

You'll walk along shaded trails, listening to bird songs and enjoy a typical Costa Rican lunch in Pueblo Viejo de Arenal. This comprehensive day trip offers the perfect blend of nature exploration, cultural immersion, and adventure activities in one of Costa Rica's most biodiverse regions.
