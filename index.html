About Us

More than transportation. An experience with purpose. We are a family native to Monteverde, Costa Rica, who has grown up surrounded by nature, mountains, and tradition. Our project is born from a love for this land and a desire to share with visitors the beauty, culture, and authentic essence of our country. We believe that traveling is not just about moving from one place to another, but about living meaningful experiences, connecting with the environment, and supporting those who care for the territory. That’s why every service we offer is based on respect for nature and a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental education. We want every person who travels with us to take away more than just photos: to discover the history, flavors, biodiversity, and spirit of Costa Rica, accompanied by people who truly know and love this place. With us, you will not only discover destinations; you will be part of an authentic, safe, and meaningful experience. Here, you don’t just travel through Costa Rica; you travel with those who live it. Let us accompany you, show you its paths, and tell you its secrets, from the heart of the forest that saw us grow. .
